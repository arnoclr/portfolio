Privacy

This website use Google Analytics to collect usage analytics data. You can opt out by clicking on the "delcine" button at the top of the page.

For those who have activated data collection, we only record the minimum amount of data :

  • Number of users and sessions
  • Session duration
  • Operating systems
  • Device models
  • Geography
  • Current version of website

To function, certain cookies are necessary:

  • nf_ab Used to provide multiples versions of the site to all users and remember the last version displayed
  • _ga 2 years Used to distinguish users.
  • _gid 24 hours Used to distinguish users.
  • _ga_[container-id] 2 years Used to persist session state.
  • _gac_gb_[container-id] 90 days Contains campaign related information. If you have linked your Google Analytics and Google Ads accounts, Google Ads website conversion tags will read this cookie unless you opt-out.
